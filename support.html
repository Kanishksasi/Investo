FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

To cancel your Investo Premium subscription, go to your iPhone’s Settings → tap your Apple ID at the top → Subscriptions → Investo → Cancel Subscription. Cancel at least 24 hours before your next renewal date to avoid being charged. Your premium access will continue until the end of the current billing period.
Download Investo from the App Store, create an account, then tap “Upgrade” from any premium lesson or the Profile tab. Select the monthly plan and tap “Start 7-Day Free Trial.” You will not be charged until the trial ends. Cancel anytime before the trial ends to avoid charges.
On the login screen, tap “Forgot Password” and enter your email address. You will receive a password reset link from Firebase Authentication. Check your spam folder if you don’t see it within a few minutes. If you continue to have trouble, email us at sasikumark@bentonvillek12.org.
Make sure you have an active internet connection when completing lessons. Progress is saved to the cloud automatically. If you notice missing XP, try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, email us with your account email and a description of the issue.

Since all billing is handled by Apple, refund requests must go through Apple directly. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find your Investo purchase, and submit a refund request. Apple typically processes refund decisions within a few days.
